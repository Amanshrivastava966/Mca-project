Welcome to the University of North Bengal Health Center Management System! At North Bengal University, we are dedicated to enhancing the healthcare experience for our students, faculty members, and staff. The Health Center Management System is an innovative solution designed to streamline the appointment process, improve accessibility to healthcare services, and ensure a seamless experience for both patients and healthcare providers.
